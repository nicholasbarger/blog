Portfolio: Pro Team Golf League

PTGL included several custom software requirements including but not limited to:

  • Online E-Commerce website to take membership registrations.
  • Future E-Commerce apparel and product sales.
  • Digital subscriptions.
  • Custom voting-game platform called Strat.e.Golf
  • In depth player stat tracking and database design to track all golf activity down to the stroke level.
  • GPS handheld device for caddies to track and report near real-time ball placement and stroke results during tournaments.
  • Online community including blogs, message forums, and community member-member messaging.
